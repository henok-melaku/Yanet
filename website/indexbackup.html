LIYANA HEALTH CARE PLC (LHC)

LIYANA HEALTH CARE PLC (LHC) was established in 2012 G.C after 12 years of working in Hawassa and different parts of Ethiopia. We provide international standard service in Addis. With our 12 years of experience, we have treated more than two million patients per year with excellence and have been awarded five times for quality service in consecutive years.

With over 800 employees and 200 specialists, we have served over 2 million patients since our opening. Recognizing the need for advanced tertiary and quaternary level treatments in Ethiopia, we established the Yanet Internal Medicine Center, a super-specialized center.

A group of specialists and sub-specialists in internal medicine, oncology, cardiology, pulmonology, hematology, histopathology, diabetology, gastroenterology, neurology, ENT, imaging, critical care, and anesthesiology make up the team at Yanet, which aims to revolutionize medical treatment in Ethiopia......Read More.
